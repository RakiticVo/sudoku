# Research Synthesis

Turn findings into decisions.

Synthesis structure:

- Question being answered.
- Options or themes.
- Criteria for comparison.
- Evidence summary.
- Recommendation and tradeoffs.
- Next actions.

Do not hide uncertainty. Say what would change the recommendation.
