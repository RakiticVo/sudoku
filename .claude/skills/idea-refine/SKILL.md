# Idea Refine

Turn fuzzy ideas into decision-ready proposals.

Workflow:

- Restate the idea in concrete terms.
- Identify the target user, job-to-be-done, constraints, and desired outcome.
- Propose 2-3 viable directions with tradeoffs.
- Call out risks, missing information, and low-cost validation steps.
- Recommend one direction with a clear next action.

Do not jump into implementation while the idea still lacks a clear audience, outcome, or acceptance signal.
