# Brainstorming

Explore before committing.

Process:

- Inspect available context before asking questions.
- Clarify purpose, audience, constraints, and success criteria.
- Ask one focused question at a time when needed.
- Offer 2-3 approaches with tradeoffs and a recommendation.
- Convert the chosen direction into a short spec or plan input.

Do not implement while the problem is still being shaped.
