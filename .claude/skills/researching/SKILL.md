# Researching

Use source-grounded research rather than recall for unstable or high-impact facts.

Research workflow:

- Define the research question and decision it supports.
- Prefer primary sources and official docs.
- Track source quality, date, and relevance.
- Compare conflicting evidence instead of smoothing it over.
- Summarize with citations and concrete recommendations.

If evidence is thin, say so and narrow the conclusion.
