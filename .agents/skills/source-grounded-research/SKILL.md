# Source-Grounded Research

Base conclusions on evidence.

Workflow:

- Define the question and decision.
- Gather primary or high-quality sources.
- Note dates, scope, and bias.
- Compare agreements and contradictions.
- Summarize only what the evidence supports.

Use citations for claims that affect decisions.
