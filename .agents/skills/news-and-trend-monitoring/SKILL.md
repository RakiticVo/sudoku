# News And Trend Monitoring

Monitor for decisions, not endless feeds.

Define:

- Topic, scope, and time window.
- Trusted sources and excluded sources.
- What counts as a meaningful signal.
- Frequency of review.
- Output format: brief, alert, table, or recommendation.

Always include dates and avoid treating early rumors as confirmed facts.
